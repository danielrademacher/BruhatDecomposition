1 Foreword

Let G be the SL, SU, Sp or SO. Let g be an element in G. We want to write g = u_1 ⋅ w ⋅ u_2 with u_1 and u_2 lower unitriangular matrices and w a monomial matrix.

This is already implemented for:

  • Special linear group (SL) [2]

  • Special unitary group (SU) [3]

  • Symplectic group (Sp) [4]
